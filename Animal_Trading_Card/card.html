Miniature Dachshund

The first dog to be successfully cloned in the UK was a dachshund named Winnie.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 13.7 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Habitat: Warm blankets

"Dachshund" is the German word for "badger dog", which is exactly what these long-bodied small dogs were bred to hunt for hundreds of years! The stubborn dogs generally don't like the rain or being told what to do.
